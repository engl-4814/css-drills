Fun things to do at Walt Disney World!

If you have never been to Disney World, you need to go! It is fun for people of all ages to enjoy. There are plenty of food options, entertaining features, amussement park rides, etc.!

Cinderella's castle is among one of the most significant views at Walt Disney World in Orlando, Florida. Some of the other important views include the new Star Wars park and the Avatar park in Animal Kingdom.

There are four different theme parks at Disney World. They are Epcot, Magic Kingdom, Hollywood Studios, and Animal Kingdom. They also have two water parks called Blizzard Beach and Typhoon Lagoon.

Park Map:

The parks are all over a large piece of land, so there are plenty of transportation services that can be used to get from one park to another. The resorts can also be seen on the map if you want to see how far away certain hotels are from each resort. Many people like to stay close to Magic Kingdom due to the firework show at the end of the night.

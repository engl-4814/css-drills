What Plants Kate is Growing

This season, Kate has 15 plants! All of the plants were seeded on March 3, 2021. She hopes for them all to grow big and strong

Kate has planted all of the following:

  • 2 pots of marigolds,
  • forget-me-nots
  • lavender,
  • cayenne peppers,
  • jalepaneos (definitely spelled this wrong),
  • habenaro peppers,
  • 2 pots of lilies,
  • carrots,
  • tomatoes,
  • basil,
  • oregano,
  • and some succulents.

She loves hanging out alone to support social distnacing and the NRV area, but before she had her plants she would sometimes be lonely in her room. Now that she has her plant friends, life is perfect! You know what they always say:

If you can't buy a dog, buy a plant, you broke loser!
